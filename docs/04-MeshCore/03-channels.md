# Michigan Channels

These are the public hashtag channels in use across Michigan's MeshCore mesh. Join them to connect with others in your area or with shared interests.

## How to add a channel

1. Open the MeshCore app
2. Tap the three-dot menu
3. Select **Add Channel**
4. Choose **Join a Hashtag Channel**
5. Enter the channel name

The channel key is automatically calculated from the hashtag name.

## Statewide channels

| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `#michmesh` | General MichMesh chat, statewide |
| `#michmesh-testing` | Test messages, diagnostics, "can anyone hear me" checks |
| `#michmesh-emergency` | Emergency and severe weather coordination |

## A note on channel etiquette

Public channels are visible to everyone in range. Keep testing brief, use `#michmesh-testing` for range checks, and remember that airtime is shared — the more people on a channel, the more important it is to keep messages short.
