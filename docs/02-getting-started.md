# Getting Started

Welcome to MichMesh. This page gets you from "what is this?" to "I'm on the network" in about 30 minutes of hands-on time.

## What you'll need

One LoRa radio and a smartphone. That's it. Radios start around $35 and work anywhere in Michigan — no service plan, no SIM card, no monthly fee.

## The five steps

1. **Pick a protocol.** MichMesh supports MeshCore, Meshtastic, and Reticulum. If you're new, start with MeshCore — it's what most of Michigan runs and it's easier to get onto an established network. See [Choosing a Protocol](./choosing-a-protocol) for the full breakdown.

2. **Buy a radio.** For Michigan's climate, we recommend nRF52840-based boards like the LilyGo T-Echo or RAK4631. See [Hardware Recommendations](./MeshCore/hardware) for the full list.

3. **Flash the firmware.** Plug the radio into your computer, open [flasher.meshcore.co.uk](https://flasher.meshcore.co.uk) in Chrome or Edge, and follow the prompts. Takes about five minutes.

4. **Install the app.** MeshCore app on iOS or Android. Pair over Bluetooth.

5. **Join the network.** Set your name, pick the USA/Canada preset, send your first message. Ask in Discord if you don't see any repeaters in range — someone nearby can help.

## Need help?

The fastest path to a working radio is our Discord. Post what you bought and where you're trying to join from — someone will walk you through it live.
